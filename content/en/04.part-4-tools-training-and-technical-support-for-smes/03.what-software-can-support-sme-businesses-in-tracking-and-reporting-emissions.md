# What software can support SME businesses in tracking and reporting emissions?

Software solutions for tracking and reporting greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions for Small and Medium-sized Enterprises (SMEs) can be categorized into three main groups, ranging from basic tools to specialized domestic and international platforms.

## Basic and free tools

For businesses just beginning their emissions tracking journey, readily available tools offer an accessible starting point.

### Microsoft Excel

As the most popular and accessible option, Excel allows businesses to create customized spreadsheets for recording operational data (e.g., electricity, fuel consumption) and applying calculation formulas based on standards like the GHG Protocol.

***Free templates***

Many organizations provide free, pre-built reporting templates. The GHG Protocol's website offers a suite of cross-sectoral calculation tools in Excel format, which serve as an essential reference for the initial stages of inventory development.

There are also reports built by Foleys[97] and KLINOVA[98] as an example.

## International specialized software:

For businesses with larger operational scales or more complex value chains, specialized international software platforms offer automated and comprehensive solutions.

***Leading platforms***

Software such as Salesforce Net Zero Cloud, Persefoni, Watershed, and Sphera can automate data collection, track emissions across all three scopes, and generate reports compliant with various global standards like the GHG Protocol, CDP, and TCFD.

While powerful, the subscription costs and implementation complexity of these platforms can present a barrier for SMEs in their early stages of carbon management.

## Domestic software solutions

Vietnam's climate technology (climate tech) sector is emerging, with several domestic providers developing solutions tailored to the local regulatory context and business environment.

***National platform (Forthcoming)***

The most significant tool will be the National Registry System, mandated by Decree 119/2025/ND-CP and operated by the Ministry of Agriculture and Environment. This official platform will become the standard for reporting and managing emissions data for regulatory compliance in Vietnam.

***Current domestic providers***

In the interim, several Vietnamese tech companies and startups have launched platforms to support businesses:

*VertZéro (by FPT IS)*[99]: A domestic platform designed to help businesses collect data, measure, and report GHG emissions in accordance with international standards.

*Nuoa.io*[100]: An ESG technology platform tailored for Vietnamese businesses, enabling the tracking and reporting of environmental metrics, including GHG emissions.

Depending on their scale, budget, and readiness level, SMEs can select the appropriate tool to either begin or advance their carbon emissions management journey, starting with simple spreadsheets and progressing toward more sophisticated, integrated platforms as their needs evolve.
